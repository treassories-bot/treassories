Shipping Policy

We offer complimentary shipping on all orders across India. Orders are typically delivered within 3–5 business days of confirmation, depending on your location. You will receive updates on your order status via email.

Cash on Delivery

Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for most pin codes. Please ensure someone is available at the delivery address to make payment upon receiving your order.

Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped by contacting us via our Contact Us page. Once an order has been dispatched, it cannot be cancelled, but may be eligible for return once delivered.

Return & Exchange Policy

We offer a 7-day return and exchange window from the date of delivery. To be eligible for a return, items must be unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase.

Refund Processing

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval status. Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method (for online payments) or via bank transfer (for COD orders).

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the product, and we will arrange a replacement or refund at no additional cost.

Contact Us

For any shipping or refund related questions, please visit our Contact Us page.
